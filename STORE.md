Record tab audio and microphone with AI transcription. Custom prompts let you summarize, translate, or extract key points automatically.

TabTalk Recorder captures audio from your browser tabs and microphone, then uses Google Gemini AI to transcribe and process your recordings exactly how you want.

KEY FEATURES:
• AI-Powered Transcription - Convert speech to text using Google Gemini
• Custom AI Prompts - Summarize meetings, translate languages, extract action items, or create any custom output
• Dual Recording - Capture both tab audio and microphone simultaneously
• Crash Recovery - Never lose recordings even if browser crashes
• Privacy First - All data stays on your device, no cloud storage

AI TRANSCRIPTION FEATURES:
• Multiple Gemini Models - Choose between Flash and Pro models
• Custom System Prompts - Tell the AI exactly how to process your audio
• Post-Processing - Automatically format, summarize, or analyze transcriptions
• Long Audio Support - Handles recordings of any length with smart chunking
• Resume Capability - Continue interrupted transcriptions
